### Language structures

***Question:*** **'What colour is it?' (De que cor é?)**  
***Answer:*** **'It's ..*colour*.. .' (É ..*cor*.. .)**

[![dewc](/images/dewc.png)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YyFLBTTAbSE)

***Answer:*** **' The ..*Object*.. is ..*Colour*..' (O ..*Objecto* é ..*Cor*..)
